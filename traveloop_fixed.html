✦ Platform Features

EVERYTHING
YOU NEED

01
🔐
Login & Signup

Secure entry point to manage your personal travel universe. Email auth, validation, and instant access.

02
🌐
Dashboard

Central command hub. Upcoming trips, popular cities, quick actions, and budget highlights at a glance.

03
✈️
Create Trip

Initiate your adventure with a name, dates, and description. Upload a cover photo. Begin the dream.

04
🗂️
My Trips

All your journeys in one place. Cards with dates, destinations, and edit/view/delete controls.

05
🗺️
Itinerary Builder

Add cities, assign dates and activities, reorder stops. Build your day-wise plan interactively.

06
📅
Itinerary View

Day-wise timeline or calendar layout. City headers, activity blocks with time and cost. Toggle views.

07
🔍
City Search

Discover destinations with country info, cost index, and popularity. Filter by region, add instantly.

08
🎭
Activity Search

Browse sightseeing, food tours, and adventures. Filter by type, cost, and duration. Enrich every stop.

09
💰
Budget Tracker

Estimated costs broken down by transport, stay, meals, and activities. Charts, daily averages, alerts.

10
🧳
Packing Checklist

Organize essentials by category — clothing, documents, electronics. Check off items as you pack.

11
🔗
Share Itinerary

Public URLs, social media sharing, copy-trip option. Inspire the world with your journey.

12
📔
Trip Journal

Notes and reminders per trip or stop. Hotel check-in info, local contacts, day-specific thoughts.
